Framework Alignment

Microsoft CAF for AI Agents

  • Plan: Business case validated; agents classified by CAF categories
  • Govern: Responsible AI controls, data governance, risk management
  • Build: Copilot Studio primary; Power Platform for automation
  • Manage: Monitoring via Power BI, alerting, lifecycle management

Microsoft AI Decision Framework (BXT)

  • Business: Clear KPIs and measurable outcomes defined
  • Experience: Conversational (Teams, chatbot) + event-driven
  • Technology: SaaS-first (Copilot Studio) before PaaS (Foundry)
