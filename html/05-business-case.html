Business Case Proposal

AgentCare — Return on investment through clinician capacity recovery and clinical quality

WHY

KPIs Impacted

  • -35% Clinician admin time per consultation (documentation automation)
  • -95% Documentation turnaround (minutes → seconds)
  • >95% Triage accuracy (urgency classification via NHS 111 knowledge)
  • -40% Phone call volume reduction (self-service triage)
  • >90% SOAP note completeness (all sections validated)

Business Impact

Clinicians spend significant time on documentation during and after consultations. AgentCare drafts SOAP notes from consultation transcripts, extracts clinical entities, and manages triage and scheduling — recovering ~15 minutes per hour of clinician time, enabling 1-2 extra patient appointments per clinician per day.

Illustrative scenario — 5-GP practice:
  • GP documentation time saved: ~£80k/year (35% of £228k admin cost)
  • Extra revenue: ~£60-100k/year (1-2 extra appointments/GP/day)
  • Better audit compliance with structured SOAP notes and full audit trail

What AgentCare Delivers

Reducing cognitive load

SOAP drafted from transcript, entity extraction — clinician focuses on the patient, not the screen

NHS-grounded triage

Urgency classification using NHS 111 knowledge source — consistent, evidence-based triage at scale

Documentation quality

Complete, structured SOAP notes from consultation transcripts — validated before saving

Full audit trail

Every AI recommendation, clinician decision, and SOAP approval logged — compliance by design
