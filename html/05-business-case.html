Business Case Proposal

AgentCare — Return on investment through clinician capacity recovery and clinical quality

WHY

KPIs Impacted

  • -50% Clinician admin time per consultation
  • -95% Documentation turnaround (minutes → seconds)
  • -80% Triage to appointment time (hours → minutes)
  • +20-30% Guideline adherence (from ~70% to >90%)
  • >85% Missed referral catch rate (new capability)
  • -40% Phone call volume reduction
  • >95% Drug interaction detection rate
  • -70% Patient referral query calls

Business Impact

Today’s clinical workflows are fragmented — clinicians, admin, and patients all compensate for disconnected systems. This creates information bottlenecks, constant micro-delays, and a failure to surface early warning signals. AgentCare recovers 20+ minutes per hour of clinician time, enabling 2-3 extra patient appointments per clinician per day.

The clinical safety net (drug interactions, guideline gaps, missed referrals) makes evidence-based practice the default. Self-service triage replaces the phone bottleneck — the #1 source of patient frustration in primary care.

Illustrative scenario — 5-GP practice:
  • GP admin time saved: ~£114k/year (50% of £228k admin cost)
  • Extra revenue: ~£100-150k/year (2-3 extra appointments/GP/day)
  • Reduced locum dependency, fewer adverse events, better patient satisfaction

BREAK EVEN: Payback is driven primarily by clinician time recovery. At a 5-GP practice, annual savings (~£114k) plus extra revenue (~£125k) far exceed ongoing costs (~£24k/yr). Implementation cost (£40k) is recovered within 3-5 months. At multi-practice scale, implementation cost is amortised further and ROI accelerates.

Figures are illustrative for a typical 5-GP UK primary care practice. Actual costs vary by region, practice size, and licensing agreements.
